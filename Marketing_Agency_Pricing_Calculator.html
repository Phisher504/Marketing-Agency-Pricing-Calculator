    • How do our design tiers work?  

    • Each tier includes the number of designs, photos, and copy that can be delivered each month.

      If you select design tier 2, each month you can receive upto

      • 2 Designs (2 of any of the below)
        • A design is any of the following:
          • A+ Content
          • Brand Story
          • Brand Store
      • +8 photo designs for secondary images
      • +6 copy updates on 6 ASINs
        • Titles / bullets

      The tiers also dictate how many design projects can be worked on at the same time.

      Example: Design tier 4 = 2 concurrent projects. Work will not start on a 3rd project until you as the client have signed off on a project fully. This is to encourage clients to give sturdy and specific direction early in the creative process to save resources and limit revisions. It rewards clients with faster usable deliveries too.

      Definition:

      Active: What is currently being worked on. Cannot exceed active project count simultaneously or must upgrade design tier.

      Requested: The number of projects that can be requested or worked in a given month.

      Design: A+ content, a brand store, or a brand story.

      Copy: A title or 5 bullet points.

      Images: Graphics, infographics, images, above the fold outside of A+ content. (Secondary images)

      Videos: Editing a video for a detail page or advertisement.

    • Explanation Of Brand Management Options  

    • The Brand Manager position facilitates core MAG services of PPC, SEO, Design, Catalog merchandising, and troubleshooting. They serve as the main point of contact with clients.

      The seniority of managers explained:

      Jr Brand Manager: Less seasoned BM to reduce your fees.

      Brand Manager: Standard Account Manager.

      Senior Brand Manager: Seasoned Account Manager, top 10% performing staff.

      Elite Brand Manager: Our best of the best, and they have reduced account burdens to give you elite service. You will receive on-demand daily attention. Want us to fly out and meet you? Done. White glove service.
